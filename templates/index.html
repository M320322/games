Halving Game

Start with a number and take turns to either subtract 1 or divide by 2. The player who reaches 0 wins!

Rules:

  • Players take turns
  • On your turn, either subtract 1 or divide by 2
  • You can only divide by 2 if the number is even
  • The player who makes the number 0 wins

Tic-Tac-Toe

Classic 3x3 grid game. Get three in a row to win!

Rules:

  • Players take turns placing X and O
  • Player 1 is X, Player 2 is O
  • First to get 3 in a row wins
  • If the board is full with no winner, it's a draw
Play Tic-Tac-Toe

Connect Four

Drop pieces into columns to get four in a row! Choose between 4x4 or 5x5 boards.

Rules:

  • Players take turns dropping pieces into columns
  • Pieces fall to the lowest available position
  • First to get 4 in a row wins (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
  • Choose between 4x4 or 5x5 board size
Play Connect Four

Nim

Strategic game with piles of objects. Remove any number from a single pile each turn.

Rules:

  • Players take turns removing objects from piles
  • On your turn, remove any number from a single pile
  • The player who takes the last object wins
  • Customize pile sizes or use default (1, 3, 5, 7)
